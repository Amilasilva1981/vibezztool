Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

How our tools handle your data

All tools on VibezzTool (font generator, counters, password generator, color tools, hashtag helper, image converter) run entirely client-side in your web browser using JavaScript. Text you type, images you upload, and passwords you generate are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to, or stored on, our servers.

Information we do collect

Like most websites, we use standard web analytics to understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are viewed and general location at a country level). This helps us improve the tools. We do not require account creation, so we do not collect names, emails, or passwords through the tools themselves.

Cookies and advertising

We work with third-party advertising partners (including Adsterra) to display ads that help keep every tool free. These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Third-party links

Our site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.
