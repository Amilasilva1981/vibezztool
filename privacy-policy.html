Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

How our tools handle your data

All tools on VibezzTool (font generator, counters, password generator, color tools, hashtag helper, image converter) run entirely client-side in your web browser using JavaScript. Text you type, images you upload, and passwords you generate are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to, or stored on, our servers. VibezzTool does not require account creation, so we do not collect names, emails, or passwords through the tools themselves.

Information we do collect

Like most websites, we use standard web analytics (such as pages viewed, general country-level location, browser type, and device type) to understand how visitors use the site and to improve it. This is aggregate usage data, not personal data tied to your identity.

Cookies and interest-based advertising

We work with third-party advertising partners (including Adsterra and, where applicable, Google) to display ads that help keep every tool free. These partners may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites, and to measure ad performance.

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. You can also opt out of personalized advertising from participating companies at optout.aboutads.info or manage Google's ad settings directly at adssettings.google.com.

Children's privacy

VibezzTool does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our tools are general-audience utilities intended for creators, bloggers, and business users. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email below and we will remove it.

Data security and your rights

Because our tools process your text, images, and passwords locally in your browser rather than on a server, there is nothing for us to store or lose in the first place. For the limited aggregate analytics data we do collect, we take reasonable measures to keep it secure, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You may contact us at any time with questions about what limited data we hold or to request more information.

Third-party links

Our site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent to hardesilva@gmail.com.
