How to Get Fancy Fonts for Your Instagram & TikTok Bio

A step-by-step look at using stylish Unicode fonts to make your social bio stand out — and why they work almost everywhere.

Open the tool →

Why it's worth using

Stand out in a crowded feed

Most bios use the same default font. A styled name or tagline catches the eye in the half-second someone scans your profile.

Works almost everywhere

These are real Unicode characters, not images — so they paste cleanly into Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter/X, and most chat apps.

No app or account needed

Unlike keyboard apps that ask for permissions, this runs in your browser and produces plain text you copy and paste.

How to use it

  1. Open the Bio Font Generator and type your name, tagline, or bio text.
  2. Scroll through the 16 generated styles — Bold, Italic, Script, Circled, Upside-down, and more.
  3. Click "Copy" next to the style you like.
  4. Paste it directly into your Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook bio field.

Pro tip

Stick to one styled font for your name and keep the rest of your bio in plain text — mixing too many styles can make a bio harder to read, especially for screen readers.
